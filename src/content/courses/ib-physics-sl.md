## Course Overview

IB Physics Standard Level provides a broad understanding of physics principles, emphasizing the nature of science and the scientific method.

## Core Topics

### 1. Measurements & Uncertainties
- Fundamental and derived units
- Significant figures and error analysis
- Propagation of uncertainties

### 2. Mechanics
- Kinematics and dynamics
- Work, energy, and power
- Momentum and impulse
- Uniform circular motion

### 3. Thermal Physics
- Temperature and thermal energy
- Ideal gas law: $PV = nRT$
- Specific heat capacity: $Q = mc\Delta T$

### 4. Waves
- Wave properties and behavior
- Standing waves and resonance
- Doppler effect: $f' = f\frac{v \pm v_o}{v \mp v_s}$

### 5. Electricity & Magnetism
- Electric fields and Coulomb's Law
- Circuits and Ohm's Law: $V = IR$
- Magnetic fields and forces

### 6. Atomic & Nuclear Physics
- Atomic structure and spectra
- Radioactive decay: $N = N_0 e^{-\lambda t}$
- Nuclear reactions and binding energy

## Internal Assessment

Students complete an individual investigation (IA) — a hands-on lab experiment with written report, contributing 20% to the final grade.
